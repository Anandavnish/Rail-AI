About Traditional IRCTC Booking

The traditional IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) platform provides direct train ticket booking services. Users can search for trains between two stations, view availability, and book tickets.

Limitation: Traditional systems only show full-route availability. If a train shows "Waitlist" status, there's no automated suggestion for alternative routing or split journeys.
